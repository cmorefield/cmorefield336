Wired: Information at the Speed of Light

Overview Hardwired Unwired The Future


Wired connections make up the backbone of our internet. Massive amounts of cables each capable of Gigabits of information persecond are necessary to keep everything running


Most of internet traffic that is routed worldwide is put through fiber-optic cables. This internet backbone is routed on land as well as a web across the sea.

This criss-crossing web allows us to enjoy the almost instant internet speeds we enjoy today.
