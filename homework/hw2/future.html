Communication has come an amazingly long way since the first electric signals were sent over telegraph. Now, lets try to look into the future a bit and see what new technologies are out there.


Quantum physics is one of the most complex fields which seems to defy our understanding of normal physics at every turn.

One very interesting concept in Quantum mechanics is quantum entanglement, where a change in one particle is directly and equally changed in another

This has the potential for instantaneous data transmission, no matter the distance.
